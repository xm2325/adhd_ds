# Metric dictionary

| Metric | Definition | Exclusions | Owner to confirm | Decision use |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Referral-to-contact | First successful contact minus referral receipt | Rejected referrals reported separately | Operations | Referral-processing capacity |
| Referral-to-assessment | Completed assessment minus referral receipt | Incomplete cases remain in backlog reporting | Operations and clinical | Patient experience and backlog |
| Acceptance-to-assessment | Completed assessment minus acceptance | Rejected referrals | Operations | Post-acceptance flow |
| Attendance rate | Attended divided by attended plus did-not-attend | Provider and patient cancellations | Operations | Reminder and booking design |
| Backlog | Accepted referrals without completed assessment at a stated snapshot | Snapshot date must always be shown | Operations | Capacity planning |

A metric definition is not approved merely because the SQL or Python runs. The owner, source of truth, exclusions, effective date and version must be recorded before production reporting.
