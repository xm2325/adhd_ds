# Decision register

| ID | Decision | Owner | Evidence required | Status in this demo |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| D01 | Primary waiting-time start is referral receipt or referral acceptance | Operations and clinical lead | Source definitions and patient-experience goal | Open |
| D02 | An extra weekly assessment clinic is feasible | Operations and finance | Staffing, cost, room and clinical constraints | Scenario only |
| D03 | Weekly outreach capacity for additional contact | Operations | Staff time, channel limits and workflow design | Synthetic default of 100 |
| D04 | Appointment-support model can enter workflow testing | Data science, clinical and governance | Calibration, group checks and prospective workflow test | Not approved; synthetic only |

A slider change or model output is not a decision. A production register should include the decision date, approver, versioned evidence, assumptions, review date and reversal condition.
